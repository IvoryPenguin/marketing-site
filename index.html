WHO WE ARE

In 2010, Ivory Penguin started as an education software consulting firm. Spurred by our love for learning, we wanted to create technology that promoted continuous education. But over the years, it became clear that we had a passion for other things, too. We love startups, penguins, kittens, non-profits, people, design, and, well, everything. We love building delightful software that is fun, sexy, and easy.

So, in order to do what we do best, we branched out. We collected more amazing people who understand our values. Our talented team has designed apps, developed websites, and deployed software to servers. Whatever we're doing, it's something we love. And this is how we came to be. This is who we are: a software firm that funnels their love for everything into loveable software for you.

CRAFTSMANSHIP IS PARAMOUNT

Raising the bar of professional software development by practicing it and helping others learn the craft.

OUR VALUES

Here at Ivory Penguin, we do more than just value software craftsmanship. We live by it. Craftsmanship is a part of us. We believe that software should not only work but be well-written, valuable, and perhaps most importantly, loveable.

Loving what you do and what you build is absolutely paramount in launching successful companies. We really take that to heart when building new products. If we wanted to be an average software development company, we could build you a minimum viable product. But we know that ordinary doesn't cut it. So instead, we build minimum loveable products - software that is lovingly made and lovingly used. We love crafting fantastic user experience and beautifully designed software, and every day we strive to become better at our craft.

Continuous learning and growth is central to what we do. Our team believes that education is wonderful and should be accessible to everyone. That's why sharing is so important. We want the world to know and share our passion for technology and learning.

EDUCATION IS KEY

Collaborative learning and growth is something to share with the world.
