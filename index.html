WHO WE ARE

In 2010, Ivory Penguin started as an education software consulting firm. Spurred by our love for learning, we wanted to create technology that promoted continuous education. But over the years, it became clear that we had a passion for other things, too. We love startups, penguins, kittens, non-profits, people, design, and, well, everything. We love building delightful software that is fun, sexy, and easy.

So, in order to do what we do best, we branched out. We collected more amazing people who understand our values. Our talented team has designed apps, developed websites, and deployed software to servers. Whatever we're doing, it's something we love. And this is how we came to be. This is who we are: a software firm that funnels their love for everything into loveable software for you.

CRAFTSMANSHIP IS PARAMOUNT

Raising the bar of professinal software development by practicing it and helping others learn the craft.

PASSION. LOVE. CRAFTSMANSHIP.

Here at Ivory Penguin, we not only value software craftsmanship, we live by it. We eat, sleep, breath craftsmanship. It courses through our veins. We believe software should not only work, but be well written, valuable and finally, lovable.

Loving what you do and loving what you build is absolutely paramount in launching successful companies. When building new products, we really take that to heart. We could build minimum viable products, but why when we can build minimum lovable products? We love crafting delightful user experience and beautiful software, we strive every day to continuously learn, grow, and become better at our craft.

Continuous learning and growth is very important to us -- we also believe in sharing. We think education is wonderful and should be available and accessible to everyone. We want to share our passion and love for technology and learning with the world.

EDUCATION IS KEY

Collaborative learning and growth is something to share with the world.
